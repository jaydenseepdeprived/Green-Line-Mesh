# GREEN LINE MESH CONSTITUTION

## Meta Rules

We hold the belief that in organizing any community-driven project, it is crucial to have facilitators and roles with a given amount of organizational "power" and avenues for anyone to challenge/refine the organization of the project. Projects die out because they cannot meet the demands of an ever evolving community of contributors and people. This is why we have "meta rules" to facilitate changes in how we organize as a community-driven project.
